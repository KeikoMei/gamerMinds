Our services

At Gamer Minds, we believe in delivering a complete package. Discover a fusion of accuracy and artistry, designed to elevate your game's global journey.

Translation

Experience unparalleled linguistic services with our expert team, proficient in a vast array of gaming genres. From RPGs to action-packed shooters, our translators master the gaming lexicon from “grinding” to “griefing.” Dive into immersive narratives with translations that capture the essence of each game, whether you're navigating the gritty streets in “Grand Theft Auto” or mastering combos in “Devil May Cry.”

Proofreading and Editing

Our meticulous proofreading and editing services ensure that your gaming content is flawless. Whether it's a nostalgic title on the Sega Genesis or the latest action-adventure for the Xbox Series, our editors skilfully fine-tune your content. With a sharp eye for detail, we iron out inconsistencies, refine gameplay instructions, and perfect narratives to enhance the gamer's experience.

Localization Quality Assurance

Elevate the gaming experience with our Localization Quality Assurance. Our LQA services focus on cultural adaptation, ensuring that every “quest” and “power-up” feels native to your audience. From the strategic depths of “Age of Empires” to the fast-paced brawls of “Super Smash Bros.,” we validate every in-game text, interface, and narrative to resonate with players globally, adhering to industry standards such as ESRB ratings.
